Karen's Favorite Restaurants

Where to enjoy the best restaurants

I love to eat! Fortunately for me there are lots of places to enjoy a great meal in St. Louis. I will talk about my four go-to favorites.

  • Wildflower Restaurant.
  • Peno Italian Soul Food
  • Forks and Stix
  • Tamm Avenue Bar

Wildflower Restaurant

The Wild Flower Restaurant & Catering was created in 1995 by owner, Tracy Czarnec. Her vision was to bring together American gourmet dining with a casual atmosphere. The decision to open in the Central West End seemed natural. A trendy area with a cosmopolitan feel, the West End turned out to be the perfect place for the Wild Flower and diners looking for more than just another restaurant.

Peno Italian Soul Food

Small, easygoing eatery offering Southern Italian soul cuisine such as seafood pastas & stews.

Fork and Stix

Stir fry rice noodles with green onions, bean sprouts, eggs, sun-dried shrimps, tofu, fish sauce, tamarind. ... Northern Thai egg noodle curry soup. ... Green beans, bell pepper, onion, soy sauce, Thai chili paste and Queen of Siam basil, serve with rice.

Tamm Ave. Grill

Hands down best burger in St. Louis, weekly specials that are always changing.
